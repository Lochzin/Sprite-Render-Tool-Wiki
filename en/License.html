License

This page summarizes the license and commercial terms for the Sprite Render Tool, based on LICENSE.md.

Note: This document is an explanatory summary. For legal accuracy and full wording, always refer to the actual LICENSE.md shipped with the add‑on.

1. Nature of the License

The Sprite Render Tool add‑on:

  • Follows the legal compliance requirements imposed by the Blender API and GPL.

  • Provides the rights that are mandatory under that compliance (e.g. access to the source code when required by GPL conditions).

On top of that, there are Commercial Distribution Terms which regulate:

  • How official builds are sold and distributed.

  • Access to updates and support for paying customers.

These commercial terms do not remove rights granted by GPL; they regulate the purchase and access to official versions, not the legal status of the code itself under GPL.

2. Commercial Distribution Terms (Summary)

2.1 Individual License

  • Intended for 1 user.

  • Allows both personal and professional use.

  • Redistribution of the purchased file as a commercial product is prohibited, even if GPL allows code redistribution.

  • Sharing the purchased download (for example by posting the original file or Gumroad link) violates the purchase agreement and may cause loss of access to support/updates.

2.2 Studio / Commercial License

  • Intended for studios, teams, or multiple artists.

  • Allows installation for up to 5 users per Studio License (extensions on request).

  • Includes priority support from the author.

2.3 Updates

  • Buyers receive access to updates while the product is actively maintained.

  • Third‑party redistributions (even when legally allowed under GPL) do not include guaranteed support or update access.

2.4 Support

  • Support is provided exclusively to official buyers.

  • Redistributed versions (e.g. downloaded from someone else) do not come with official support.

3. Warranty and Liability

According to LICENSE.md:

  • The add‑on is provided “as is”, without any kind of warranty.

  • The author is not responsible for direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages resulting from the use of this add‑on.

  • The author may change, pause, or discontinue:

    • future support,

    • development,

    • update frequency, at any time.

4. Important Notes

  • The commercial terms do not restrict rights that are guaranteed by GPL compliance (for example, code rights that follow from using Blender’s API under GPL).

  • They do regulate:

    • who is considered an official customer,

    • who is entitled to support,

    • who receives updates via the official distribution channel.

  • Violating the commercial terms (e.g. sharing your purchase) can result in:

    • revoked access to future updates,

    • loss of official support.

5. Author Statement

From the original LICENSE.md:

  • The add‑on is created by Gabriel “Loch” Bonani (Lochzin).

  • It is aimed at:

    • supporting multi‑camera rendering,

    • enabling fast and efficient export of animation frames for 2D games and other creative projects.

By purchasing the tool you help support independent development and further improvements to the add‑on.
